Adorable Guinea Pigs

Guinea Pigs are cute little rodents that have found homes as popular American pets! They are relatively small, only about twice the size of a hamster,
but are packed full of adorable features! Many are used in class rooms because of their docile nature to teach kids how to handle a pet. They are also
much more durable than their hamster counter-parts! Their diets consist mainly of leaves and vegetables. Alfalfa hay is a favorite! Guinea pigs are sure
to make excellent pets for any household!

  • Low maintenence!
  • Fun to play with!
  • Makes cute noises!
